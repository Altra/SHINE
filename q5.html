3. A Teacher standing in front of students holds a speaker that produces a note of constant frequency and swings it around in a circle. What happens to the note heard by the students as the speaker moves away from them?(1)

Show

Answer

The Pitch decreases.

Accept frequency decreases, note becomes deeper/lower. Do not accept 'note decreases'. Ignore 'quieter'

Examiner's Remarks

The sound becoming quieter was a common incorrect response. No credit was given for 'it decreases' or 'the note decreases' since it was not clear enough to what the student referred. Correct answers were rare.
